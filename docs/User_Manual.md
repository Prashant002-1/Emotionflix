# EmotionFlix User Manual

## Welcome to EmotionFlix!
EmotionFlix is an emotion-based movie recommendation platform that suggests films based on how you're feeling. Instead of browsing endless categories, simply tell us your mood and we'll find movies that match perfectly.

---

## How EmotionFlix Works

### The 7 Core Emotions
EmotionFlix recognizes **7 core emotions** on a 0-100% scale:
- **😊 Happy** - Joy, excitement, contentment
- **😢 Sad** - Sorrow, melancholy, disappointment  
- **😠 Angry** - Frustration, irritation, rage
- **😨 Fearful** - Anxiety, nervousness, apprehension
- **🤢 Disgusted** - Revulsion, distaste, aversion
- **😮 Surprised** - Shock, amazement, astonishment
- **😐 Neutral** - Calm, balanced, no strong emotion

## Finding Movies by Your Mood

### Method 1: Quick Recommendations (All Users)
**Recommendations Page - Instant Movie Discovery:**
1. Navigate to "Recommendations" in the main menu
2. Use the emotion sliders to set how you're feeling
3. Watch as movie recommendations update in real-time
4. Adjust emotions to explore different movie types
5. Click on any movie to see full details

**Slider Tips:**
- Drag sliders to adjust each emotion from 0-100%
- Emotions automatically normalize to total 100%
- Try different combinations to discover new genres
- Higher emotion percentages have stronger influence

### Method 2: Movie Match Feature (All Users)
**Find Your Perfect Movie Match:**
1. Go to "Movie Match" page
2. Search for specific movies or browse popular titles
3. See your compatibility percentage with each movie
4. Adjust your emotional profile to see how matches change
5. Discover why certain movies match your current mood

### Method 3: Advanced Emotion Detection (Account Required)
**Log Page - Precise Emotion Capture:**

#### Using Your Webcam
1. Click "Use Camera" on the Log page
2. Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
3. Position your face clearly in the frame
4. Click "Capture Photo" after the 1-second countdown
5. ML model analyzes your facial expression instantly
6. Review detected emotions with confidence indicator
7. Accept results or manually adjust if needed
8. Get personalized recommendations based on detected emotions

**Best Results:**
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Look directly at the camera
- Keep your face clearly visible and unobstructed
- Use a neutral background if possible

#### Uploading a Photo
1. Click "Upload Photo" on the Log page
2. Select a clear photo of your face from your device
3. Wait for ML model analysis (1-2 seconds)
4. Review detected emotions and confidence score
5. Adjust manually if needed
6. Get recommendations based on analysis

**Photo Tips:**
- Use recent, clear photos with good lighting
- Face should take up most of the image
- Avoid sunglasses or anything covering your face
- JPG and PNG formats are supported

#### Manual Emotion Entry
1. Click "Manual Entry" on the Log page
2. Use sliders to set each emotion from 0-100%
3. See real-time preview of your emotion distribution
4. Adjust until it matches how you're feeling
5. Save your emotional profile
6. Get recommendations based on your input

**When to Use Manual Entry:**
- ML model confidence is low (<40%)
- You want precise control over emotion mix
- Camera/photo upload isn't available
- Exploring different emotional combinations

## Understanding Confidence Scores

### What Confidence Means
The ML model provides a confidence score for emotion detection:

- **High Confidence (>70%)**: 🟢 Green indicator - ML model is very confident in the results
- **Medium Confidence (40-70%)**: 🟡 Yellow indicator - Good results but manual review suggested
- **Low Confidence (<40%)**: 🔴 Red indicator - Manual input recommended for better accuracy

### Why Confidence Matters
- Ensures you get accurate movie recommendations
- Alerts you when manual adjustment might help
- Helps maintain recommendation quality
- Gives you control over the final emotional profile

## Privacy & Security

### Data Protection
- **Local Processing**: Emotion detection happens on your device, not on servers
- **No Image Storage**: Photos are never saved or transmitted to servers
- **Minimal Data Collection**: Only essential information for functionality

### Camera & Photo Permissions
- **Camera Access**: Only granted when webcam detection chosen
- **Revocable**: Can be disabled in browser settings anytime
- **Transparent**: Clear indicators show when camera is active
- **Secure**: All processing happens locally in the browser

### Account Security
- **Secure Passwords**: Encrypted and hashed password storage
- **JWT Authentication**: Industry-standard secure login tokens
- **HTTPS**: All communications encrypted in transit

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Issues
**Camera Not Working:**
- Check browser permissions for camera access
- Close other apps that might be using your camera
- Refresh the page and try again
- Use photo upload or manual entry as alternatives

**Poor Emotion Detection:**
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Position face clearly in frame
- Remove sunglasses or face coverings
- Try multiple photos for better results


### Account & Technical Issues
**Login Problems:**
- Verify you're using the correct email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" if needed
- Clear browser cookies and try again
- Contact support if issues persist

**Slow Loading:**
- ML model models load on first use (normal delay)
- Subsequent uses are much faster
- Check internet connection for movie data
- Try refreshing the page
